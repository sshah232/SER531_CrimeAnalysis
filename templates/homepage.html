About the Project: Globally, tourists and strangers are usually ignorant of how crimes can affect them when they move into cities. Therefore, a crime intelligence monitoring program, involving the use of geo-tagged data and user profiles for the provision of real-time information on how a crime hot spot is likely to shift with regard to the specific location is proposed. This study focuses on personal security and the creation of friendly city environments in search of an overall response to improving urban safety using OWL and SPARQL.
